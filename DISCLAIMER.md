## Intended use

- For learning, evaluation, and building your own compliant data pipelines.
- The code targets **publicly accessible** pages only — no authentication bypass, no access to private, gated, or paywalled content.

## Before you scrape any site

- **Read the Terms of Service and `robots.txt`** of the target site, and respect them. Some sites prohibit automated access; honor that.
- **Do not collect personal data** protected under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws. If a use case touches personal information, get a lawful basis first.
- **Do not redistribute whole datasets** that may be protected by database rights or copyright. Extract what you need; don't republish someone else's corpus.
- **Rate-limit your requests** so a target site is never degraded. Throttle, back off, and keep concurrency low.
- **Respect intellectual property** — facts are generally not copyrightable, but creative content, images, and compilations may be.
